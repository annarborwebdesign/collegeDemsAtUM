Who Are We?

JustDems is the social justice committee of College Democrats. Our committee works to raise awareness about social justice issues and how they impact both students on this campus, as well as citizens across the country. Our work includes creating pamphlets on health-care, to writing letters to Congress encouraging them to support the rights of immigrants and refugees, to actually having our members go to Lansing and lobby members of the Michigan Legislature. We are always looking for new members to our committee. If you are interested in doing rewarding work that makes a real impact, JustDems is the perfect place for you!
