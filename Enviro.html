Who Are We?

EnviroDems is the environment issue committee of the College Democrats at the University of Michigan. We meet weekly from 7-8pm on the fourth floor (red couches) of the Union. In our meetings, we discuss environmental policy, work on projects, and fight for environmental policy change. See you there!
