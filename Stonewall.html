Who Are We?

Stonewall Democrats is the LGBTQ+ issues committee of College Democrats. We advocate for friendly policies, politicians, and communities. In the past we have done events to emphasize LGBTQ+ people in the workforce with Jim Toy, a coming out speak out, and friendly voter guide. We meet every Tuesday at 9 p.m. on the red couches on the fourth floor of the Union.
